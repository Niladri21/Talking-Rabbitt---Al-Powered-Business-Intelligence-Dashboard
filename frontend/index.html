Our Mission

To make business intelligence as easy as texting a colleague. We are building the future of conversational BI, empowering everyone to ask questions and get instant, accurate charts without relying on complex pivot tables or data teams.

The Problem We Solve

Traditional BI tools have a massive learning curve. People export data back to spreadsheets just to understand it. We bridge that gap using large language models that understand your context and generate visual insights on the fly.
